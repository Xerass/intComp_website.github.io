The History of Our Pride

Emerging from the mystical forests of a remote Chinese village, the legend of the "Le Goût De L'eau" began when enchanted vines producing goblet-shaped fruits were discovered. Embracing the whimsy, villagers cultivated these magical vines, believing them to be a tipsy deity's gift. Over centuries, under the reign of the eccentric Prince Estrella of the Six Orders, the shop evolved into the most outrageously opulent luxury drinks destination. With elixirs distilled from water guarded by an ancient dragon and decor featuring fountains of ambrosia, Le Goût De L'eau became a global pilgrimage site for those seeking a sip of absurdity, transforming a mystical forest grove into a ludicrously luxurious urban haven.
